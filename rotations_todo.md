# Podstawowe Rotacje DPS - MoP 5.5.3 (Throne of Thunder)

## Priorytety Implementacji

### Tier 1 - Popularne DPS specs (Do implementacji w pierwszej kolejności)

#### **Warlock - Affliction**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Maintain Agony, Corruption, Unstable Affliction on target
2. Use Dark Soul: Misery on cooldown
3. Haunt on cooldown (if talented)
4. Malefic Grasp as filler
5. Drain Soul below 20% HP
6. Summon Doomguard/Terrorguard for burst

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- DoT uptime (Agony, Corruption, UA) > 95%
- Haunt uptime > 90%
- Soul Shard management
- Dark Soul usage

#### **Warlock - Demonology**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Maintain Corruption and Doom
2. Enter Metamorphosis with 900+ Demonic Fury
3. In Meta: Touch of Chaos spam
4. Out of Meta: Soul Fire with Molten Core procs
5. Hand of Gul'dan for AoE
6. Dark Soul: Knowledge on cooldown

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Metamorphosis uptime
- Molten Core proc usage
- Doom uptime > 95%
- Demonic Fury waste

#### **Warlock - Destruction**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Maintain Immolate
2. Conflagrate on cooldown
3. Chaos Bolt with Backdraft or high Burning Embers
4. Incinerate as filler
5. Dark Soul: Instability + trinkets for burst
6. Rain of Fire for AoE (3+ targets)

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Immolate uptime > 95%
- Conflagrate usage
- Burning Embers waste
- Backdraft proc usage

#### **Hunter - Beast Mastery**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Bestial Wrath on cooldown
2. Kill Command on cooldown
3. Arcane Shot to dump focus
4. Cobra Shot as filler
5. Stampede for burst
6. A Murder of Crows on cooldown

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Bestial Wrath uptime
- Kill Command casts
- Focus capping
- Pet uptime 100%

#### **Hunter - Marksmanship**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Careful Aim phase (>80% HP): Aimed Shot spam
2. Chimera Shot on cooldown
3. Kill Shot when available
4. Aimed Shot with Master Marksman proc
5. Steady Shot as filler
6. Rapid Fire for burst

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Chimera Shot usage
- Master Marksman proc waste
- Careful Aim usage
- Kill Shot usage

#### **Hunter - Survival**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Maintain Serpent Sting
2. Explosive Shot on cooldown
3. Black Arrow on cooldown
4. Arcane Shot to dump focus
5. Cobra Shot as filler

### Tier 2 - Średnio popularne specs

#### **Mage - Arcane**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Arcane Blast to 4 stacks
2. Arcane Missiles with proc
3. Arcane Barrage at 4 stacks (mana management)
4. Evocation when low mana
5. Arcane Power + trinkets for burst
6. Nether Tempest/Living Bomb maintenance

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Arcane Charge management
- Mana management
- Arcane Missiles proc usage
- Cooldown alignment

#### **Mage - Fire**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Maintain Living Bomb/Pyroblast
2. Combustion with DoTs up
3. Pyroblast with Heating Up/Pyroblast! proc
4. Inferno Blast to convert Heating Up
5. Fireball as filler
6. Alter Time for double Combustion

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Hot Streak proc usage
- Combustion timing
- Living Bomb uptime
- Heating Up conversion

#### **Death Knight - Frost (DPS)**
**Priorytet rotacji:**

2. Obliterate with Killing Machine
3. Frost Strike to dump Runic Power
4. Howling Blast for AoE/Rime proc
5. Pillar of Frost on cooldown
6. Soul Reaper below 35%

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Disease uptime > 95%
- Killing Machine proc usage
- Runic Power capping
- Pillar of Frost usage

#### **Death Knight - Unholy**
**Priorytet rotacji:**
1. Outbreak for diseases
2. Dark Transformation on cooldown
3. Scourge Strike as main attack
4. Festering Strike to refresh diseases
5. Death Coil to dump Runic Power

**Kluczowe metryki:**
- Disease uptime > 95%
- Dark Transformation uptime


### Priorytety metryk:
1. **DoT/Buff uptime** - najważniejsze dla większości specs
2. **Proc usage** - nie marnowanie Hot Streak, Killing Machine, etc.
3. **Resource management** - nie cap'owanie energii/many/focus
4. **Cooldown usage** - używanie major cooldowns
5. **Combo point/Holy Power waste**
